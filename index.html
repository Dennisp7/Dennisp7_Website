Interests

Apart from education, I spend most of my time learning and teaching. I have dedicated my extracurricular activities to working with others and showing them the fruit of perseverance. For example, I have volunteered with the Special Olympics program for special needs individuals in the Central Valley to provide training for an all-day sports tournament. I taught my team that they are free to pursue any dream they want and to enjoy whatever they are doing. As a result, my team successfully won two championship games and, most importantly, had fun. I have also worked with veterans and helped them transition to civilian life. I strived to provide them with meaningful resources and concrete directives to accomplish their goals. As a result, most of the veterans I worked with have pursued higher education or attained meaningful work. Lastly, I leveraged my knowledge of programming languages and front-end web development to provide non-traditional students at UC Merced with an engaging and informative website. As a result, students I worked with have been exposed to resources they were not aware of before. One student, in particular, was able to successfully navigate issues with being undocumented. I later helped that student obtain a job in my department so that they could assist other students in the same way.

As far as personal interests, I enjoy spending time with my family. I also enjoy hiking, playing sports, mechanics, and watching tutorial videos on mathematics and physics. Although, my favorite thing to do is interact with and learn the perspectives of others.
